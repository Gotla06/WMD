Meet Our Trainers

Kagiso Molefe

Strength & Conditioning Specialist

With 10 years of experience, Kagiso specializes in strength training, powerlifting, and functional fitness.

  • Certified NASM Personal Trainer
  • Specializes in: Strength Training, Powerlifting
  • "Strength doesn't come from what you can do. It comes from overcoming the things you once thought you couldn't."

Amantle Kgosi

Yoga & Mobility Coach

Amantle brings 8 years of yoga expertise with a focus on mobility, flexibility, and mindfulness practices.

  • RYT 500 Certified Yoga Instructor
  • Specializes in: Yoga, Mobility, Recovery
  • "Yoga is not about touching your toes, it's about what you learn on the way down."

Thabo Ramotswe

CrossFit & HIIT Specialist

Thabo is a competitive CrossFit athlete who brings high-intensity training to all fitness levels.

  • CrossFit Level 2 Trainer
  • Specializes in: CrossFit, HIIT, Endurance
  • "The only bad workout is the one that didn't happen."
